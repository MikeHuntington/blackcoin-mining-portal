How do I Start Mining

BC Pool (BCP) doesn't require user registration. Getting started is super simple. Just point your miner at our pool using your BlackCoin address as username and anything as password:

stratum+tcp://bcpool.co:3032 -u YOUR_BC_ADDRESS -p anything

I'm Connected, Now What?

Just sit back and let the BlackCoins roll in. You can always checkup on your stats by viewing the My Miner section of the website.

http://www.bcpool.co/miner/YOUR_BC_ADDRESS

Payouts are made daily between 12am-2am UTC (8pm-10pm EST)
